Introduction

At MsgMeAI, operated by Dwija LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service, including:

  • Personal Information: First and last name, email address, telephone number, and other information you provide during registration.
