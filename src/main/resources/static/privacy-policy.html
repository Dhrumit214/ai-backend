EXPRESS CONSENT

By checking this box, you explicitly acknowledge and consent to the following:

  • Consent is not required to use our service or to make any purchases.
  • Messages may be sent via automated dialing technology.
  • You consent to receive messages even if your phone number is listed on a "Do Not Call" registry.
  • You understand that message frequency and charges apply as described above.
  • You can easily opt out at any time.
HOW TO OPT OUT

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message from msgmeai.com
  • Adjusting your preferences directly in your account settings
  • Contacting our customer support team
AFTER OPT-OUT

Once opted out, you will receive a confirmation message indicating that you have been unsubscribed. No further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.
